## What I Read

[There are millions of books](https://isbndb.com/blog/how-many-books-are-in-the-world/) plus a near unlimited supply of content available via the internet. 

1. Filter out the slop https://etymology.substack.com/p/against-content. As a general principle, I won’t read anything generated by AI as I assume little to no effort was put in. 
2. Follow links and citations - Knowledge is a web and certain authors and concepts come up again and again. For example, schismogenesis. 
3. Find authors I trust
4. Get recommendations
5. Seek out opposing viewpoints
